Triaxial apparatus at Aalto University

The GDS Triaxial Automated System (TAS) has been in use since 2018. The system consists of two stations. The station number 1 (left side) is equipped with a remote feedback module for accurate loop control. Experiments with this system can be referred to: (i) Results of triaxial tests on intact HUT clay 2019-2020.

Centrifuge at University Gustave Eiffel

The beam centrifuge at Gustave Eiffel University (formerly IFSTTAR, L.C.P.C.) was founded in 1985. It has been used in simulating the lateral response of monopiles for wind turbine foundations.
