Research highlights

My research focuses on the geotechnical aspects of renewable energy infrastructure. I study soil-pile interactions in offshore wind turbines and erosions in earth dams. I employ a combination of elementary laboratory tests, centrifuge modelling & numerical analysis to address the challenges of sustainable energy infrastructure under complex environmental loads. I believe in vision & action.
